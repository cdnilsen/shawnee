##Loyal Shawnee Rules##


#(Notebook #25, text #139; obtained from Frank Daugherty. From a handwritten copy only--no free translation is given.)#

#Chief advises his own people. Tell young boys and girls, e.g., at any stomp ground where there are several in other dances. Keep repeating from time to time, for fear that young people would violate the rules. Howekeela ni=yeesi-mhseeleyakwe howeenoki.#
