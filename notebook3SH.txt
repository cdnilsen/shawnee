V--43 F.D. Doctored in the Sweat House
Long ago in the past when I was a child, I became sick (and) I was doctored. My mother summoned an old man. Then I was doctored by that (old man and) made to go in a sweat house twice. When he came thereafter, that's what my mother is told by him: "This boy will become well but I myself will own him. You (and your husband) must give (your boy) to me. I will not take him away. I will just be a friend to him. Probably the kind of man I am,--that's the kind of man he will be in future time. That's why we ask for him: I see him (prophetically)[:] that he will be depended on by many old men so that they may derive benefit from him because he will always keep everything which he hears. That's why I will be a friend to him: I see him (prophetically).

197. How the Claw got in the Bundle.
197.1.S: peeleko laakwa taʔpiyeči=lenaweewiwaači paaši kileki paʔta=wilawiiki kileki noʔki mata θapa wiyehi taʔwiiša=waakoteleči
197.1.G:
197.1.E: At one time as they [the Shawnee] lived there, they were apparently almost destructive; and also presumably they did not [possess] anything at the time to get knowledge.

197.2.S: hiini waači paalohi pelʔhamowaači hiini taʔwaakotamowaači
